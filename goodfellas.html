"GoodFellas" The Movie

Custom Poster design and Advertising Movie Premiere

This project focused on designing a customized Goodfellas poster, website hero, and Instagram post with a strong visual identity. The goal was to translate a classic film aesthetic into modern, eye-catching designs using Adobe Photoshop, Illustrator, and Lightroom. Throughout the design process, I created most visual elements from scratch and incorporated a small number of locally sourced images, prioritizing consistency, visual impact, and polished presentation across all deliverables.
